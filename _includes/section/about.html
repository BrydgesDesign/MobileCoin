About

Payment technology designed for you

MobileCoin is payments technology built for you. In a perfect world, money would be secure, fast, and almost invisible. This is the story of how a few people banded together to summon the future they imagine into the present so that we could live in a better tomorrow.

Whether you are spending or saving, the most important thing about money is trust. We designed MobileCoin with the best security available today so you can trust that your money is right where you expect it to be. When you want to pay for something, you want it now. MobileCoin transactions complete in seconds. When you want to pay for something, you don't want to be tracked, unless you consent. It is money that puts your privacy first, designed to be used by people like you for every transaction, every day.

Digital Money

It seems inevitable that digital money will eventually replace physical currency. At MobileCoin, we believe governments have a legitimate interest in regulating the economic lives of their citizens.

Good monetary policies help keep economies healthy and currency controls help fight criminals and terrorists. Our job is to create high performance digital payments technology that can transparently encompass the tradeoffs that societies choose.
