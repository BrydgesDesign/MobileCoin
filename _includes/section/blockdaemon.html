Blockdaemon

(Infrastructure)

Blockdaemon is the leading blockchain node infrastructure platform internally ISO 27001 aligned with enterprise-grade security and backup systems.

Supporting 30+ protocols, 70 out-of-the-box node types, and monitoring plug-ins for cloud and on-prem services, Blockdaemon is used by exchanges, custodians, enterprises, financial institutions and 10k+ developers to connect commercial stakeholders to blockchains.

Their mission is to simplify the process of deploying nodes and creating scalable enterprise blockchain solutions via APIs, BYOI, auto-decentralization and auto-healing of nodes.

Blockdaemon will run most of the nodes in MobileCoin network on top of Microsoft Azure.
