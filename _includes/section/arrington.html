Arrington XRP Capital

Arrington XRP Capital is a cryptocurrency venture fund that was founded by Michael Arrington and Heather Harde.

They’ve amassed a large crypto-portfolio, and we’re appreciative of their support.

The firm had this to say about joining the MobileCoin Foundation:

“Arrington XRP Capital is excited to be part of the MobileCoin Foundation. As early supporters of the team, we have been impressed with MobileCoin’s secure and super fast transactions. The future of payment is mobile and increasingly embedded in the most secure messaging apps. The MobileCoin Foundation will play a big role and making that future real for millions of consumers worldwide."
