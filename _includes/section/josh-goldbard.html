Joshua Goldbard

We’d like to announce Joshua Goldbard as the final member of our MobileCoin Foundation board.

Joshua is the CEO of MobileCoin Inc. and the co-inventor of the MobileCoin protocol. Prior to MobileCoin, Joshua was Managing Director of a crypto hedge fund. Joshua likes subwoofers.
